# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Credit Underwriters, Loan Officers, and Lending Managers analyzing borrower risk, setting risk policies, and validating bank statement authenticity.

## Product Purpose
FinScope provides a zero-database, in-memory, privacy-centric credit underwriting dashboard. It parses bank statement PDFs (including password-protected and scanned files via Groq Vision OCR) to construct a double-entry ledger, analyze balances, calculate risk profile scores, detect potential anomalies, and generate underwriting reports.

## Brand Personality
Clinical, expert, high-reliability, and clean. Emphasizes accuracy, trust, and professional efficiency without distraction.

## Anti-references
- Saturated 'warm-neutral' SaaS templates (cream/beige/sand background colors, generic orange/purple decorative gradients).
- Over-animated, distracting, or gamified financial tracking UIs.
- Cramped, low-contrast, low-density legacy corporate banking panels.

## Design Principles
1. **Expert Confidence & Clarity**: Layouts should present dense financial ledger data with clear typographic hierarchy, avoiding visual fluff.
2. **Clinical Contrast & Readability**: Ensure body text and secondary labels meet or exceed WCAG AA contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1) against dark/surface backgrounds.
3. **No-Dadaism / Purposeful Decoration**: Avoid decorative layout animation, glowing glassmorphism, or non-functional shadows. Colors and highlights must communicate risk levels (e.g. green for pass, red for decline).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict adherence to WCAG AA contrast guidelines.
- Responsive scaling preserving layout boundaries without content bleed on various screens.
- Keyboard navigation compatibility and clear visible focus rings.
